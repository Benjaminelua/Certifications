## ℹ️ About NYSC
The **National Youth Service Corps (NYSC)** scheme was established by the Nigerian government to promote national unity and development. Graduates from Nigerian universities and polytechnics are required to participate in the program for a period of one year.

Key activities during the service year include:
- Community development service
- Professional work experience
- National integration initiatives
